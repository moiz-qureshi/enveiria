Meet the Team

Shahraiz Qureshi

San Jose, California | CEO

Shahraiz Qureshi is the co-founder and CEO of Enveiria. He has previously worked with the co-founder of Darkstore, Axlehire and Nootroo. He completed his Bachelors of Science in Chemical Engineering with emphasis on business development from San Jose State University. He has ample experience in working with corporate companies such as Veeva, PSC Biotech and Graphic Packaging International. He believes that the mission of Enveiria is to offer opportunities for success to dedicated and talented students. “ Empowering students to succeed gives me a great deal of excitement. It sometimes feels unattainable, but with the right attitude, skills and tools, success is evidently within their reach.”

Zeshawn Nazir

Lahore, Pakistan | COO

Zeshawn A. Nazir is the Co-Founder and COO of Enveiria. He also co-founded Sherry’s Signature Events: a company specialized in the planning and organization of exceptional events for private and corporate clients. Zeshawn excels in the process of events from conception to execution. Furthermore, he has helped Clarkson University, NY, to raise funds for academic expansion. After moving to Silicon Valley, the technical hub of the world and having prior experience of living in Lahore, the business hub of Pakistan, Zeshawn observed the youthful talent in Pakistan and the lack of opportunities at their dispense. “ I want to create opportunities for the exciting prospects to have the experience of professional internships. I will make that happen through Enveiria.”

Moiz Qureshi

San Francisco, California | CTO

Moiz Qureshi is the co-founder and CTO of Enveiria. He also previously co-founded and is the director of Universal Mercy Academy, a nonprofit school. He is an alumni of Year Up: a professional development organization to engage youthful grown-ups in achieving their potential through career and education opportunities. He has a strong background in technology and entrepreneurship. Moreover, he has worked with VC based companies including iCracked and Eden. In addition, he is a Infrasructure Security Analyst at MuleSoft: a Salesforce company. “ I am blessed to have hit many successful milestones in my journey, but my ultimate goal and passion is to assist others in finding success. Enveiria will be a key factor for student’s connection between reward and accomplishment.
