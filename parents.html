Welcome, Parents & Advisors!

We have created this page to answer any questions and address any concerns that you may have about sending your adult abroad. Going to another country for an internship is a huge step for your child’s personal growth and professional development.From the application process to their arrival, Enveiria will be with your child every step of the way. We only send hand pick selected qualified individuals to complete internships abroad, thus have making sure they get undivided attention and make sure they are safe while having the time of their life and expanding their horizon of success.
Always putting the interns first

Career Benefits

In today's competitive job market, students must find ways to differentiate themselves from their peers. The career benefits of an international internship are essential for young people seeking to advance their professional futures. After completing their internship, your child will have had international exposure in a globalized world and professional experience.
