The Enveiria Platform

Enveiria seeks to provides its clients the most streamlined experience possible. Keeping in mind the multiple logistical planning, we have designed a application mechanism to obtain maximum data with minimal effort and increased conversions.
At Enveiria, we feel that students already have a full plate of academics and extracurricular. Thus, many students shy away from the thought of finding an internship let alone figure out all the logistics.
Therefore, we have developed this comprehensive platform which easily connects higher educations students to workplaces within SIlicon Valley.
